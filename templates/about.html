Understanding Respiratory Conditions

Learn about COVID-19 and Pneumonia Detection

COVID-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Most people infected with the virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment.

Common Symptoms:

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough and shortness of breath
  • Fatigue and body aches
  • Loss of taste or smell

Viral Pneumonia

Viral pneumonia is an infection of the lungs caused by viruses. The infection causes inflammation in the air sacs of the lungs, leading to breathing difficulties and other symptoms.

Key Characteristics:

  • Inflammation of air sacs (alveoli)
  • Fluid accumulation in lungs
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Chest pain and cough

Comparison of Conditions

Feature COVID-19 Viral Pneumonia
Cause SARS-CoV-2 virus Various respiratory viruses
X-ray Appearance Ground-glass opacity, bilateral infiltrates Patchy or diffuse infiltrates
Onset Gradual onset over days Can be rapid or gradual
Treatment Approach Supportive care, antivirals if severe Antiviral medication, supportive care
