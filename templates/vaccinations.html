COVID-19 Vaccination Information

Stay protected with up-to-date vaccination guidance

Available Vaccines

Pfizer-BioNTech

  • Type: mRNA vaccine
  • Doses: 2 shots, 21 days apart
  • Efficacy: 95% after second dose
  • Age Group: 12 years and older

Moderna

  • Type: mRNA vaccine
  • Doses: 2 shots, 28 days apart
  • Efficacy: 94.1% after second dose
  • Age Group: 18 years and older

Johnson & Johnson

  • Type: Viral vector vaccine
  • Doses: Single shot
  • Efficacy: 72% overall
  • Age Group: 18 years and older

Vaccination Process

1

Check Eligibility

Verify your eligibility for vaccination based on your age and health conditions.

2

Schedule Appointment

Book your vaccination appointment through your local healthcare provider or vaccination center.

3

Get Vaccinated

Receive your vaccine dose and wait for the observation period.

4

Follow Up

Schedule and receive your second dose if required.

Important Information

Side Effects

Common side effects include:

  • Soreness at injection site
  • Fatigue
  • Headache
  • Muscle pain
  • Fever or chills

After Vaccination

  • Continue wearing masks
  • Maintain social distancing
  • Practice good hygiene
  • Monitor for side effects
  • Keep vaccination card safe
