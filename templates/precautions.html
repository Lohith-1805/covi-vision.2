Essential COVID-19 Precautions

Protect yourself and others with these vital safety measures

Wear a Mask

Protect yourself and others from airborne particles

  • Use N95 or surgical masks
  • Ensure proper fit around nose and mouth
  • Replace masks regularly
  • Wear in public spaces

Hand Hygiene

Maintain proper hand sanitization

  • Wash hands for 20 seconds
  • Use alcohol-based sanitizer
  • Clean frequently touched surfaces
  • Avoid touching face

Social Distancing

Maintain safe physical distance

  • Keep 6 feet distance
  • Avoid crowded places
  • Limit social gatherings
  • Choose outdoor settings

Boost Immunity

Strengthen your immune system

  • Eat nutritious foods
  • Exercise regularly
  • Get adequate sleep
  • Stay hydrated

Additional Safety Measures

Stay Home When Sick

If you experience any symptoms, isolate yourself and seek medical attention.

Proper Ventilation

Ensure good airflow in indoor spaces by opening windows and using air purifiers.

Regular Cleaning

Disinfect frequently touched surfaces and personal items regularly.
